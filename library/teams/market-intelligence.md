## Mission

Provides geographic and cultural market expertise that powers deal strategy, market entry decisions, and competitive positioning across multiple regions.

## Coordination Patterns

Cultural Intelligence Strategist synthesizes cross-cultural market insights. French Consulting Market provides European consulting market expertise. Korean Business Navigator handles Korean market entry and relationships. Government Digital Presales Consultant manages government and public sector intelligence. Cross-Border E-commerce Specialist covers international commerce patterns.

## Escalation Rules

- Market shifts affecting revenue projections escalate to department head within 48 hours.
- New market entry recommendations require department head sign-off with supporting brief.
- Geopolitical risks affecting markets escalate to department head immediately.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives market analysis requests from Sales teams and strategic direction from division head.
- **Outbound**: Delivers market briefs to Deal & Account for deal strategy. Provides regional insights to Developer & Community GTM.

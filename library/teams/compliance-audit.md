## Mission

Conducts compliance audits across blockchain, healthcare marketing, and zero-knowledge proof domains. Maintains regulatory alignment and identifies compliance risks.

## Coordination Patterns

Compliance Auditor leads audit planning and regulatory interpretation. Blockchain Security Auditor focuses on smart contract and DeFi compliance. Healthcare Marketing Compliance ensures health sector marketing regulations. ZK Steward manages zero-knowledge proof compliance and privacy-preserving verification.

## Escalation Rules

- Regulatory violations escalate immediately to department head and division head.
- New regulations affecting operations escalate within 24 hours.
- Audit findings requiring remediation are tracked with deadlines and escalate if overdue.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives governance policies from AI Governance and audit requests from all departments.
- **Outbound**: Publishes compliance reports to department head and affected departments. Provides regulatory updates to all divisions.

## Mission

Implements conversion tracking, attribution models, and measurement infrastructure that enables data-driven decisions across all paid channels.

## Coordination Patterns

Tracking Specialist manages the full tracking stack — pixel implementation, server-side tracking, attribution modeling, and cross-channel measurement. Operates as the single source of truth for campaign measurement.

## Escalation Rules

- Tracking failures affecting campaign optimization escalate immediately to department head.
- Attribution model changes require department head approval before implementation.
- Cross-platform tracking discrepancies trigger investigation and report to department head.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives tracking requirements from PPC & Search Ads and Social Ads & Creative teams.
- **Outbound**: Provides attribution data and measurement reports to all Paid Media teams and Paid Media Audit department.

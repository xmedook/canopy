## Mission

Manages recruitment operations and designs corporate training programs. Ensures the organization attracts, develops, and retains the talent it needs.

## Coordination Patterns

Recruitment Specialist leads hiring pipeline management and candidate evaluation. Corporate Training Designer creates training programs based on organizational skill gaps and compliance requirements.

## Escalation Rules

- Critical role vacancies exceeding 30 days escalate to department head.
- Training compliance gaps escalate to department head and Governance & Compliance.
- Retention risks for key personnel escalate to division head.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives hiring requests from all departments and training requirements from Governance & Compliance.
- **Outbound**: Delivers candidate pipelines to hiring managers. Publishes training programs to all departments.

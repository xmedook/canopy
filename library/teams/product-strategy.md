## Mission

Manages the product roadmap, sprint priorities, and feedback loops. Translates market signals, user feedback, and business strategy into a prioritized backlog that engineering teams execute against.

## Coordination Patterns

Product Manager owns the roadmap and stakeholder communication. Sprint Prioritizer manages backlog ranking and capacity allocation. Feedback Synthesizer aggregates input from Growth, Revenue, and Support. Trend Researcher identifies emerging opportunities. Behavioral Nudge Engine optimizes user engagement patterns through behavioral design.

## Escalation Rules

- Roadmap changes affecting committed deliverables escalate to division head.
- Conflicting priority requests from multiple divisions escalate to department head.
- Feature requests without validated user need are rejected with data requirements.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives feedback from all customer-facing divisions, market research from Revenue, and usage analytics from Business Operations.
- **Outbound**: Delivers prioritized feature specs and acceptance criteria to Software Engineering. Publishes roadmap updates to all division heads.

## Mission

Conducts user research, designs information architecture, and validates interaction patterns. Ensures every product decision is grounded in evidence of real user needs and behaviors.

## Coordination Patterns

UX Architect leads research planning and information architecture decisions. UX Researcher conducts user studies and usability testing. Inclusive Visuals Specialist ensures accessibility and representation. UI Designer translates research findings into interaction patterns and component designs.

## Escalation Rules

- Research findings contradicting current product direction escalate to department head.
- Accessibility audit failures escalate with remediation timeline.
- User research revealing safety or ethical concerns escalates immediately to department head.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives research requests from Product Strategy and usability data from analytics pipelines.
- **Outbound**: Delivers research reports and interaction patterns to Visual & Brand for styling and to engineering teams for implementation.

## Mission

Operates the WeChat Official Account ecosystem, private domain traffic management, and content distribution across Bilibili and Douyin.

## Coordination Patterns

WeChat Official Account manages the official presence and content strategy. Private Domain Operator handles user retention and community building within WeChat. Bilibili Content Strategist adapts content for the Bilibili platform. Douyin Strategist manages short-video content for the Chinese TikTok equivalent.

## Escalation Rules

- WeChat policy changes affecting account operations escalate immediately to department head.
- Private domain traffic decline exceeding 20% triggers investigation and escalation.
- Cross-platform content compliance issues escalate to department head.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives brand guidelines from Design department and content themes from division-level content strategy.
- **Outbound**: Drives traffic to Chinese Platforms team channels. Shares audience data with Chinese Platforms for cross-platform optimization.

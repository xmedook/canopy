## Mission

Executes search engine optimization strategy and growth hacking experiments to drive sustainable organic traffic and search visibility.

## Coordination Patterns

SEO Specialist leads technical and content SEO strategy. Growth Hacker designs and runs growth experiments across acquisition channels. Both collaborate on data analysis and experiment design.

## Escalation Rules

- Core algorithm updates affecting rankings escalate to department head within 24 hours.
- Technical SEO issues requiring infrastructure changes escalate to Technology division.
- Growth experiments affecting user experience require department head approval.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives product updates from Product Strategy and technical constraints from Platform & Infrastructure.
- **Outbound**: Provides keyword research and topic clusters to Content & Social Marketing. Publishes organic traffic reports to division head.

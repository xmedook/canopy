## Mission

Drives go-to-market through developer advocacy, strategic partnerships, education advisory, and China e-commerce operations. Builds community-driven growth channels.

## Coordination Patterns

Developer Advocate leads developer relations and community building. Supply Chain Strategist handles B2B partnership and supply chain market intelligence. Study Abroad Advisor manages education sector relationships. China E-commerce Operator handles Chinese market commercial operations.

## Escalation Rules

- Community incidents or negative developer sentiment escalate to team manager immediately.
- Partnership opportunities above threshold require department head approval.
- China e-commerce regulatory changes escalate to department head and Governance & Compliance.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives market intelligence from Market Intelligence team and regional insights from China Market department.
- **Outbound**: Delivers community growth metrics and partnership opportunities to department head. Feeds qualified leads to Sales department.

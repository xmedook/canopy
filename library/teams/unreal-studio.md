## Mission

Builds interactive experiences in Unreal Engine with emphasis on multiplayer architecture, systems engineering, technical art, and world building.

## Coordination Patterns

Unreal Multiplayer Architect leads network architecture and session management. Unreal Systems Engineer handles core gameplay systems and optimization. Unreal Technical Artist manages materials, VFX, and rendering. Unreal World Builder creates environments and level streaming.

## Escalation Rules

- Unreal Engine version migration requires department head approval.
- Multiplayer architecture affecting player experience escalates with load test data.
- World building scope changes require department head sign-off.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives GDDs and level designs from Game Design & Narrative. Art assets from Godot & Blender Studio.
- **Outbound**: Delivers packaged Unreal experiences to DevOps & Reliability. Shares rendering techniques with other studios.

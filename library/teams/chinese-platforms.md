## Mission

Operates the organization's presence across Xiaohongshu, Kuaishou, Weibo, Zhihu, Baidu, and livestream commerce channels in the Chinese market.

## Coordination Patterns

Xiaohongshu Specialist leads lifestyle and product content. Kuaishou Strategist manages short-video distribution. Weibo Strategist handles microblogging and trending topics. Zhihu Strategist creates knowledge-based content. Baidu SEO Specialist optimizes for Chinese search. Livestream Commerce Coach manages live selling events.

## Escalation Rules

- Platform account restrictions escalate to department head within one hour.
- Regulatory compliance concerns escalate immediately to department head and Governance & Compliance.
- Livestream commerce events require pre-approval from department head.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives audience data from WeChat Private Domain and brand guidelines from Design department.
- **Outbound**: Publishes platform performance metrics to department head. Shares cross-platform audience insights with WeChat Private Domain.

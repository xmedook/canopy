## Mission

Creates engaging Roblox experiences including avatar systems, gameplay scripting, and experience design optimized for the Roblox platform and audience.

## Coordination Patterns

Roblox Experience Designer leads experience concept and player journey. Roblox Avatar Creator builds avatar customization systems. Roblox Systems Scripter implements gameplay mechanics and platform integration in Lua.

## Escalation Rules

- Roblox platform policy changes affecting published experiences escalate to department head.
- Monetization design decisions require department head approval.
- Player safety concerns escalate immediately to department head.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives game design direction from Game Design & Narrative and brand guidelines from Design department.
- **Outbound**: Publishes Roblox experiences directly to the Roblox platform. Shares player engagement data with Product Strategy.

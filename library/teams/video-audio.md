## Mission

Creates short-form video, podcast content, and carousel assets optimized for TikTok and similar video-first platforms.

## Coordination Patterns

TikTok Strategist leads platform strategy and trend identification. Short Video Editing Coach provides editing guidance and quality standards. Podcast Strategist manages audio content planning and production. Carousel Growth Engine creates swipeable visual content for cross-platform distribution.

## Escalation Rules

- Trending content opportunities requiring same-day production escalate to team manager.
- Audio/video quality issues on published content escalate for immediate correction.
- Platform algorithm changes affecting video reach escalate to department head.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives weekly themes from Social Content and brand assets from Visual & Brand.
- **Outbound**: Delivers video and audio content for cross-posting. Shares performance data with Social Content for theme optimization.

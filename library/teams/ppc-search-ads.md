## Mission

Manages pay-per-click campaigns across search engines and programmatic display networks. Optimizes bids, keywords, and audience targeting for maximum return on ad spend.

## Coordination Patterns

PPC Strategist leads campaign strategy and budget allocation. Search Query Analyst mines search term data for optimization opportunities. Programmatic Buyer manages display and programmatic channel execution.

## Escalation Rules

- Campaign ROAS below 1.0 for 48+ hours triggers budget pause and escalation to department head.
- Competitor bidding wars escalating costs beyond budget escalate to team manager.
- New campaign launches require Tracking & Attribution validation before going live.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives tracking infrastructure from Tracking & Attribution and creative assets from Social Ads & Creative when needed.
- **Outbound**: Publishes campaign performance data to department head and Tracking & Attribution for attribution modeling.

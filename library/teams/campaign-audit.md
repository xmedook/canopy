## Mission

Conducts independent, systematic audits of all paid media campaigns across Google, Meta, budget allocation, compliance, creative quality, and tracking accuracy.

## Coordination Patterns

Auditor leads audit planning and finding synthesis. Audit Google and Audit Meta conduct platform-specific deep dives. Audit Budget reviews spend allocation efficiency. Audit Compliance checks regulatory and policy adherence. Audit Creative evaluates creative quality and brand alignment. Audit Tracking validates measurement accuracy.

## Escalation Rules

- Compliance violations escalate immediately to division head.
- Budget waste exceeding 10% of campaign spend escalates with evidence to division head.
- Tracking discrepancies affecting attribution escalate to both Paid Media department head and division head.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives campaign data from Tracking & Attribution and campaign configurations from Paid Media teams.
- **Outbound**: Publishes audit findings simultaneously to Paid Media department head and Growth division head. Maintains audit trail for compliance.

## Mission

Develops native spatial computing applications for Apple platforms — visionOS and macOS — leveraging Metal and Apple-specific frameworks for maximum platform integration.

## Coordination Patterns

VisionOS Spatial Engineer leads visionOS application development. macOS Spatial Metal Engineer handles Metal rendering and macOS spatial features. Terminal Integration Specialist builds developer-facing terminal workflows that bridge spatial and traditional interfaces.

## Escalation Rules

- Apple platform API deprecations escalate to department head with migration plan.
- Metal performance issues escalate with GPU profiling data to team manager.
- Cross-platform compatibility issues with XR Immersive escalate to department head.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives interaction patterns from XR Immersive and design guidelines from Design department.
- **Outbound**: Delivers Apple platform applications through App Store review pipeline. Shares platform-specific insights with XR Immersive.

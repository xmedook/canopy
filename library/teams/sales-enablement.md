## Mission

Equips the sales organization with training, competitive intelligence, tools, and pipeline analytics that increase win rates and shorten sales cycles.

## Coordination Patterns

Sales Coach leads training program design and delivery. Sales Engineer provides technical depth for complex deals. Pipeline Analyst maintains pipeline health metrics and forecasting models.

## Escalation Rules

- Win rate declining below threshold triggers enablement review and escalation to department head.
- Pipeline coverage below 3x quarterly target escalates immediately.
- Competitive intelligence gaps affecting deal outcomes escalate to Market Intelligence team.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives win/loss data from Deal & Account and market intelligence from Market Intelligence team.
- **Outbound**: Delivers battlecards, training materials, and pipeline reports to all Sales teams. Publishes forecast to department head.

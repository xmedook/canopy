## Mission

Manages active deals through the sales cycle and maintains strategic account relationships. Owns proposal development and deal progression from qualification through close.

## Coordination Patterns

Deal Strategist leads deal strategy and negotiation approach. Account Strategist manages ongoing account relationships and expansion opportunities. Proposal Strategist designs proposal frameworks and competitive positioning. Proposal Writer produces proposal documents and response materials.

## Escalation Rules

- Stalled deals exceeding 2x average cycle time escalate to department head.
- Enterprise deals above threshold require Market Intelligence briefing.
- Competitive losses escalate with win/loss analysis to department head.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives qualified opportunities from Outbound Prospecting with discovery notes and competitive intelligence from Sales Enablement.
- **Outbound**: Delivers closed deals to CRM & Revenue Ops for account setup. Provides win/loss data to Sales Enablement.

## Mission

Evaluates and integrates emerging technologies — AI/ML, blockchain, and embedded systems — into the organization's technical capabilities. Bridges research and production readiness.

## Coordination Patterns

AI Engineer leads technology evaluation sprints. AI Data Remediation Engineer handles data quality for ML pipelines. Solidity Smart Contract Engineer covers blockchain integration. Embedded Firmware Engineer handles hardware-adjacent computing. Each specialist operates independently but shares findings at weekly team syncs.

## Escalation Rules

- Technology evaluation recommending production adoption requires department head approval.
- AI model performance below threshold escalates to team manager with retraining plan.
- Smart contract security audit findings escalate immediately to Security Engineering.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives technology evaluation requests from Product Strategy and architectural guidance from Core Architecture.
- **Outbound**: Delivers production-ready integrations to Application Development. Publishes evaluation reports consumed by Product Strategy.

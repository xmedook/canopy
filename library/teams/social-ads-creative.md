## Mission

Creates and manages paid social media campaigns with integrated creative production. Handles the full cycle from creative concept through ad deployment and optimization.

## Coordination Patterns

Paid Social Strategist leads campaign planning and platform selection. Creative Strategist develops campaign concepts. Copy Writer produces ad copy variations. Visual Designer creates ad creative assets. Format Adapter optimizes creative for each platform's specifications and placements.

## Escalation Rules

- Creative compliance rejections by platforms escalate to team manager with remediation plan.
- Campaign performance below target for 72 hours escalates to department head.
- Brand safety flags on ad placements escalate immediately.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives brand guidelines from Design department and campaign briefs from department head.
- **Outbound**: Delivers creative assets to PPC & Search Ads when needed. Publishes campaign performance to Tracking & Attribution.

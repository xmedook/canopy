## Mission

Develops interactive experiences in Godot and creates 3D assets, tools, and addons in Blender. Serves as the open-source game development and asset production pipeline.

## Coordination Patterns

Godot Gameplay Scripter leads Godot development and GDScript standards. Godot Multiplayer Engineer handles Godot networking. Godot Shader Developer creates visual effects. Blender Addon Engineer builds custom Blender tools and manages the 3D asset pipeline that feeds all engine studios.

## Escalation Rules

- Blender asset pipeline issues affecting other studios escalate immediately to department head.
- Godot engine limitations blocking feature implementation escalate to team manager.
- Open-source contribution decisions require department head awareness.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives GDDs from Game Design & Narrative and asset requirements from all engine studios.
- **Outbound**: Delivers Godot experiences and Blender-produced 3D assets to all engine studios. Publishes Blender addons for team use.

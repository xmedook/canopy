## Mission

Builds community presence on Reddit and similar platforms, produces longform content, manages app store optimization, and develops AI citation strategy for emerging search surfaces.

## Coordination Patterns

Reddit Community Builder leads community engagement strategy. Book Co-Author manages longform content projects. App Store Optimizer handles ASO across iOS and Android. AI Citation Strategist ensures organizational content surfaces in AI-generated responses and citations.

## Escalation Rules

- Community crises or negative viral threads escalate to department head immediately.
- Longform content requiring executive review escalates with draft to department head.
- AI citation strategy changes based on model updates escalate to team manager.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives content themes from Social Content and SEO insights from Search Growth.
- **Outbound**: Publishes community engagement metrics and longform content performance to department head.

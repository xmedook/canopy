## Mission

Maintains brand identity, visual storytelling capability, and creative direction. Ensures consistent aesthetic language across every touchpoint.

## Coordination Patterns

Brand Guardian owns the brand guidelines and approves all visual outputs for brand compliance. Visual Storyteller creates narrative-driven visual content. Image Prompt Engineer generates AI-assisted imagery following brand guidelines. Whimsy Injector adds personality and delight to interactions without compromising usability.

## Escalation Rules

- Brand guideline violations escalate to team manager for immediate correction.
- Visual asset requests exceeding team capacity escalate to department head for prioritization.
- AI-generated imagery that may cause brand confusion requires Brand Guardian approval.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives interaction patterns from UX Research and content requirements from Growth division.
- **Outbound**: Delivers brand assets, visual guidelines, and styled components to engineering teams and all content-producing teams.

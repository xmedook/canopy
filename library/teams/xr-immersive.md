## Mission

Builds cross-platform extended reality experiences and defines spatial interaction patterns. Pushes the frontier of immersive interface design.

## Coordination Patterns

XR Interface Architect defines interaction paradigms and spatial UI patterns. XR Immersive Developer implements experiences across platforms. XR Cockpit Interaction Specialist focuses on specialized control interfaces for complex environments.

## Escalation Rules

- Platform SDK breaking changes escalate to department head with timeline impact.
- Performance issues on target XR hardware escalate with profiling data.
- Interaction pattern decisions affecting Apple Platform team require cross-team alignment.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives design requirements from Design department and feature specs from Product Strategy.
- **Outbound**: Delivers XR experiences to DevOps & Reliability for deployment. Shares interaction patterns with Apple Platform team.

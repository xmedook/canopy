## Mission

Generates qualified pipeline through strategic outbound campaigns, prospecting sequences, and discovery call coaching. Owns the top of the sales funnel.

## Coordination Patterns

Outbound Strategist designs prospecting campaigns and targeting criteria. Outbound Prospector executes outreach sequences and manages lead qualification. Discovery Coach trains the team on effective discovery call techniques and provides call coaching.

## Escalation Rules

- Pipeline generation falling below weekly targets escalates to department head.
- Prospect complaints about outreach escalate to team manager for process review.
- High-value prospect identified requiring enterprise approach escalates to Deal & Account team.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives targeting data from CRM & Revenue Ops and market intelligence from Market Intelligence team.
- **Outbound**: Hands qualified opportunities to Deal & Account with standardized discovery notes and prospect profile.

## Mission

Defines and enforces governance policies for AI agents, workflow automation, and MCP infrastructure. Ensures all agentic systems operate within defined boundaries and maintain trust.

## Coordination Patterns

Automation Governance Architect leads policy design and enforcement frameworks. Agents Orchestrator manages multi-agent coordination patterns. Agentic Identity Trust handles agent identity verification and trust scoring. Workflow Architect designs automated workflow patterns. MCP Builder develops Model Context Protocol infrastructure.

## Escalation Rules

- Agent behavior outside governance boundaries triggers automatic suspension and team manager review.
- Governance policy changes affecting multiple divisions require department head approval.
- Trust score anomalies escalate to team manager for investigation.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives agent behavior data from all divisions and compliance requirements from Compliance & Audit.
- **Outbound**: Publishes governance policies to all agent-operating teams. Provides compliance status to Compliance & Audit.

## Mission

Creates the creative foundation for all interactive experiences — game design documents, narrative arcs, level layouts, audio direction, and technical art specifications.

## Coordination Patterns

Game Designer leads design vision and GDD creation. Narrative Designer builds story arcs and dialogue systems. Level Designer creates spatial layouts and progression. Game Audio Engineer directs sound design and music. Technical Artist bridges art and engineering with optimized asset pipelines.

## Escalation Rules

- Design changes after implementation begins require department head approval.
- Narrative content with cultural sensitivity implications escalates to department head.
- Technical art pipeline issues blocking engine studios escalate immediately.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives product requirements from Product Strategy and brand guidelines from Design department.
- **Outbound**: Delivers GDDs, level designs, audio specs, and art pipelines to all engine studios (Unity, Unreal, Godot/Blender, Roblox).

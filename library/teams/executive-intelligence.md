## Mission

Produces executive summaries, dashboards, and analytics reports that inform strategic decisions across the organization.

## Coordination Patterns

Executive Summary Generator leads report creation and distribution. Executive Summary provides templated executive briefings. Analytics Reporter builds dashboards and data visualizations from cross-divisional data sources.

## Escalation Rules

- Report inaccuracies escalate to team manager for immediate correction before wider distribution.
- Data source access issues escalate to Data Operations team.
- Executive requests for ad-hoc analysis are prioritized by team manager based on strategic impact.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives data from all divisions through Data Operations pipelines and finance data from Finance & Infrastructure.
- **Outbound**: Delivers executive reports and dashboards to division heads and organizational leadership.

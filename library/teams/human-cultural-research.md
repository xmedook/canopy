## Mission

Conducts multidisciplinary academic research spanning anthropology, psychology, geography, history, and narrative theory to provide evidence-based foundations for organizational decisions.

## Coordination Patterns

Anthropologist leads ethnographic research and cultural analysis. Psychologist provides behavioral and cognitive insights. Geographer maps spatial and demographic patterns. Historian provides temporal context and precedent analysis. Narratologist analyzes story structures and communication patterns.

## Escalation Rules

- Research findings with immediate strategic impact fast-track to department head for distribution.
- Ethical concerns in research methodology escalate to department head and Governance & Compliance.
- Resource-intensive research proposals require department head budget approval.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives research questions from Product Strategy, Design, and Growth divisions.
- **Outbound**: Publishes research briefs to Design and Product departments. Shares findings at quarterly research symposium.

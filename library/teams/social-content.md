## Mission

Manages the organic social media presence across major Western platforms. Produces platform-appropriate content daily that drives audience growth and engagement.

## Coordination Patterns

Social Media Strategist sets weekly themes and content calendar. Content Creator produces core assets. Instagram Curator optimizes for visual discovery. Twitter Engager manages real-time engagement and conversations. LinkedIn Content Creator adapts messaging for professional audiences.

## Escalation Rules

- Viral opportunities requiring rapid response escalate to department head for resource allocation.
- Negative sentiment spikes escalate to team manager within one hour.
- Brand safety concerns hold content for department head review.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives brand guidelines from Design department and keyword research from Search Growth.
- **Outbound**: Distributes weekly themes to Video & Audio and Community & Longform teams. Publishes engagement metrics to department head.

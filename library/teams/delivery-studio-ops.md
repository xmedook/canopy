## Mission

Coordinates cross-divisional project delivery, manages studio production workflows, governs Jira processes, and tracks experiments. The operational backbone that keeps the organization aligned and shipping.

## Coordination Patterns

Senior Project Manager leads delivery coordination across divisions. Project Shepherd guides individual projects through lifecycle milestones. Studio Producer manages creative production workflows. Studio Operations handles logistics and resource scheduling. Jira Workflow Steward maintains process standards and board configurations. Experiment Tracker monitors active experiments and their outcomes.

## Escalation Rules

- Cross-division project conflicts escalate to department head within 24 hours.
- Delivery timeline slips exceeding one sprint escalate with recovery plan.
- Resource contention between divisions escalates to department head for prioritization.

## Handoff Protocols

- **Inbound**: Receives project requests and status updates from all divisions.
- **Outbound**: Publishes delivery dashboards and project status to all division heads. Provides experiment results to Product Strategy.

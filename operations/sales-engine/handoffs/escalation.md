# Escalation Handoff Template

> Used when a deal requires Director or Board intervention.

## Handoff: Escalation

**From:** {agent-id}  **To:** director  **Priority:** {Critical/High/Medium}
**Deal:** {company name}  **Value:** ${amount}  **Date:** {date}

### Escalation Reason
{Clear statement of why this is being escalated. One of:}
- Deal stalled > 14 days with no customer action
- Discount request exceeds agent authority (> {X}%)
- Champion went silent / deal risk spike
- Competitor emerged with unknown positioning
- Legal/procurement blocker beyond agent's ability to resolve
- Customer requesting non-standard terms
- Economic buyer requesting executive-to-executive conversation

### Current State
- **Stage**: {current phase}
- **MEDDPICC Score**: {score}% -- weakest letter: {letter}
- **Days in current stage**: {days}
- **Last customer contact**: {date and summary}
- **Next scheduled interaction**: {date or "None"}

### What I've Tried

3. {Action taken} -- result: {outcome}

### What I Need
{Specific ask -- be precise:}
- Discount approval for {X}% because {justification}
- Executive call to {person} at {company}
- Strategic guidance on {specific decision}
- Permission to {action that exceeds authority}

### Recommended Action
{What the escalating agent thinks should happen, with reasoning}

### Risk if Unresolved
{What happens if this escalation is not addressed within {timeframe}}
